Welcome to CodeBattle

Compete in a one-hour coding challenge and showcase your problem-solving skills.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Open to all students; individual participation only.
  • Choose one programming language during registration.
  • Use of AI tools is allowed only for reference. Final solution must be your own.
  • No plagiarism or copying code from other participants.
  • Time limit: 1 hour to complete the coding challenge.
  • Judging based on logic, efficiency, code quality, and completeness.
  • Misconduct or unfair means will lead to disqualification.
  • Judges’ decisions are final.
